We're thrilled to share some exciting news: auditions for *Puffs* are officially complete, and we've chosen our cast! Rehearsals kick off on **July 7th**, and the energy around this production is already building.

This is such a fun group to be working with. We've got some wonderful returning faces who know the Theatre17 ropes, alongside some fantastic new talent joining us for the first time. That mix of experience and fresh energy is exactly what makes a production come alive, and we couldn't be more excited to get started.

If you caught any of our auditions, thank you for coming out and sharing your talent with us — it wasn't an easy decision! And to our new cast: welcome aboard. We can't wait to dive into rehearsals with you and bring this story to life together. It's going to be a lot of fun.

**Want to get involved behind the scenes?**

We're still looking for folks to join our Creative Team — crew, design, and more. If that sounds like you, we'd love to hear from you! Reach out through the contact form on our website, or drop us a line at [theatre17@gmail.com](mailto:theatre17@gmail.com).

Stay tuned for more updates as rehearsals get underway. See you at the theatre!
