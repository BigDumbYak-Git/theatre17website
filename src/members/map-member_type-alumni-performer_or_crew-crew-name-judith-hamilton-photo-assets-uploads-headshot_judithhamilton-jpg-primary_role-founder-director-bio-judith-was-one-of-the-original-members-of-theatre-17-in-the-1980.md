bio: >-
  Judith was one of the original members of Theatre 17 in the 1980’s and when
  there was interest in resurrecting community theatre she stepped up and became
  an integral member of the new group.  She was also a member of Dryden
  Entertainment Series. 



  Judith directed many productions including our first play in 2006 “Quilters” followed by many successful productions, notably “Steel Magnolias” and “Calendar Girls”.  Her last production with Theatre 17 was “Clue”  in the spring of 2023\


  Judith will be missed by all who knew her especially the arts community.
