bio: >-
  Amy Maua has been an active member of Theatre 17 for many years and has
  enjoyed contributing both on and off the stage. Her roles include Sarah
  in *Quilters*, Marina in *Tony n Tina's Wedding*, Magenta in *The Rocky Horror
  Show*, and Barbara in *August: Osage County*. She has directed *Noises
  Off* and *Blithe Spirit*.


  In addition to performing and directing, Amy is passionate about supporting community theatre behind the scenes and currently serves as Chair of the Theatre 17 Board. She enjoys helping bring productions to life and working with fellow volunteers to keep live theatre thriving in Dryden.
